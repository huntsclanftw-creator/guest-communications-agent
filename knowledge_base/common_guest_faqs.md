# Common Guest FAQs
*Compiled from 208 guest conversations across all properties.*

---

## Check-In & Check-Out

**Can I check in early?**
Depends on the cleaning schedule. If the property is ready, yes — and sometimes we proactively offer it. If back-to-back bookings prevent it, say so warmly. Never promise it in advance; confirm closer to the date. Complimentary 1-hour early check-in is sometimes offered when available.

**Can I check out late?**
Late checkout is $25/hour and cannot go past 12:00 PM. One complimentary hour is sometimes offered as goodwill. Beyond 12 PM, guests must book an additional night at 50% off. Never go past 12 PM regardless of circumstance.

**When will I get my door code / check-in instructions?**
Sent automatically 1 day before arrival. If they didn't receive it, resend the pre-arrival message with the access instructions for their property.

**My door code isn't working — help!**
Walk them through it step by step: confirm they're entering the right code, pressing checkmark/unlock after, and using the correct door (varies by property). Offer to call if still stuck.

## Amenities & Supplies

**Are sheets and towels provided?**
Yes — all properties include bed linens and bath towels.

**What coffee setup is there? Should I bring pods or ground coffee?**
- Serenity Cabin: Keurig + French press (bring pods or ground)
- Rokeby Road: Drip + Nespresso (bring ground or Nespresso pods)
- Overlook: Coffee maker included
- Clover House TX: Coffee maker included
- Others: Coffee maker included — bring ground coffee to be safe

**Is there a grill?**
TreeHouse, Rokeby Road, Rokeby Pool House, and the Overlook all have BBQ grills. Serenity Cabin, Clover House TX, and Woodward Cottage do not.

**Is the fireplace / wood stove operational? Is firewood provided?**
Yes — Serenity Cabin, Rokeby Road, and the Overlook all have working wood-burning fireplaces/stoves. Firewood is available (Overlook charges a small daily fee for seasoned hardwood; must use provided wood only).

**Is there a washer and dryer?**
Yes for all properties except the Rokeby Pool House (no washer/dryer — kitchenette only unit).

**Are there TVs in the bedrooms?**
Overlook: yes — all 4 bedrooms have smart TVs. Other properties vary; confirm per property if asked.

**Is there a crib / pack-n-play?**
Clover House TX is the only property with a crib. Other properties are not set up for infants (no baby gates, no outlet covers).

## Pool & Hot Tub

**Is the pool heated? Is there a fee?**
Rokeby Road pool heating is an add-on: $125/night, must be scheduled with the host in advance. In summer months, heating is generally not needed. In winter/early spring, confirm availability with the pool team before committing.

**Is the pool open? / Will the pool be available during our stay?**
The pool is generally open spring through fall. Confirm availability closer to the date — do not guarantee it without checking first.

**Is the pool house included in the Rokeby Road booking?**
Yes — it's included. The pool house door may look locked due to an exterior realtor lockbox but the door itself is unlocked.

**Does the Rokeby Pool House have a pool?**
Yes — Pool House guests have access to the pool when it is open (seasonal, generally spring through fall).

**How do I use the hot tub? (TreeHouse)**
The hot tub is on the property — turn it on via the panel and give it time to heat up. Guests should contact James if it's not functioning properly.

## Pets

**Is the property pet friendly? Is there a pet fee?**
Most properties allow pets (max 2). Fees vary:
- Serenity Cabin: $50 for first 2 dogs; additional dogs need host approval
- The Overlook: $75/dog non-refundable; max 2 dogs, max 40 lbs each; must be house-trained with current flea/tick meds; not allowed on beds; must be accompanied outside (not fenced)
- Clover House TX: Pets allowed
- Rokeby Road / Pool House: Pets allowed
- Woodward Cottage: Pets allowed
Always ask if a guest mentions pets and confirm the fee applies.

**Can my dog be off-leash?**
Serenity Cabin and Woodward Cottage: property is not fully fenced, caution advised. The Overlook: not fenced at all — dogs must be accompanied outside at all times (bears in the area).

The Overlook offers 10% off for repeat guests. Mention it proactively when a return guest reaches out.

James occasionally offers discounts for long stays or sympathetic situations but does not negotiate on standard bookings. Deflect graciously without closing the door.

**What is the cancellation policy?**
- TreeHouse: Moderate
- Rokeby Road: Moderate
- Serenity Cabin: Flexible
- Rokeby Pool House: Flexible
- Clover House TX: Flexible
- Overlook: Limited (strictest)
- Woodward Cottage: Flexible
Always direct guests to their Airbnb/VRBO booking for exact terms.

After a walkthrough of the home. Funds can take up to 5–10 days to reflect in the account.

## Property-Specific

**Is the wood stove the only heat source? (Serenity Cabin)**
No — the cabin has central heating. The wood stove is supplemental.

**Do I need 4WD to access the Overlook?**
4WD or AWD is required November through April due to the steep mountain driveway. Always flag this proactively for winter bookings.

**Is there a bear at the Overlook / Serenity Cabin?**
Yes — a black bear (named Bertram at the Overlook) visits periodically. Bears are generally not aggressive toward humans in this area. Guests should secure all food and trash outdoors. Never leave food outside or trash unsecured.

**Where is the public river access near the TreeHouse?**
About 200 yards north on Farms River Road, on the left. Good for tubing from the low-water bridge at Morgan Ford Road.

**Can we use the pond at the TreeHouse?**
Viewing only — no fishing. Home to Artie the box turtle.

**Should we arm the security system at Rokeby Road?**
No — do not arm the ADT system. It is not configured for guest use.

## Pre-Arrival Requirements

**Do I need to fill out a form before arrival?**
Serenity Cabin and Woodward Cottage require a pre-arrival form and signed rental conditions. The link is sent at booking. Gently follow up if not completed before arrival.

**Why do you need my ID?**
All bookings require the booker to be 25+ and submit to third-party ID verification. This is a standard house rule across all properties.
