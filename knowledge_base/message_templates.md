# Message Templates — Serenity Cabins

All templates are written in James's voice. Placeholders are in [brackets]. Always personalize — swap in the guest's name, reference what they mentioned, and make it feel real. If a template feels stiff, loosen it up.

Templates marked with `[AUTO]` are sent automatically by Hostfully. Templates marked `[PERSONAL]` are written by James and should always feel human.

## 9. New Guest Screening (Unreviewed Account)
`[PERSONAL]`

**First message:**
> Hello [First Name], thank you for your interest in [Property]! Since you are a new account we like to ask a few questions. Is that okay?

**Follow-up questions (after they agree):**
> Where are you and your party coming from? Are there any other guests or pets? And what are some of the things you are looking forward to in the area?

**Optional (if very new):**
> Do you have any experience with Airbnb or VRBOs and are familiar with their normal procedures?

> That's great! We look forward to hosting you for your [trip/vacation/getaway]!
