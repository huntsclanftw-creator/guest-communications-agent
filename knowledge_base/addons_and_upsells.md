# Add-Ons & Upsells
*Paid extras guests can request before or during their stay.*

---

## Early Check-In
- **Fee:** $25/hour
- Available only if the property has been cleaned — confirm before offering
- Never promise in advance; confirm closer to the date
- **How to respond:** "Our standard check-in is 4:00 PM, but we can offer early check-in at $25 per hour if the property is ready. Let me check and get back to you."

---

## Late Check-Out
- **Fee:** $25/hour, available until 12:00 PM maximum
- Only available if no same-day turnover — confirm before offering
- Past 12:00 PM: guest must book an additional night (James may offer a discount)
- **How to respond:** "We can offer late check-out at $25 per hour up until noon, as long as we don't have a same-day turnover. Let me check on that for you."

---

## Pool Heating (Rokeby Road only)
- **Fee:** $125/night
- Must be scheduled with James in advance
- Payment request sent through Airbnb after confirming how many nights
- Do not guarantee availability without checking with James first
- **How to respond:** "Pool heating is available at $125 per night. Just let me know how many nights you'd like it heated and I can send a payment request through Airbnb."

---

## Extra Trash-Out
- **Fee:** $50
- Available at any property if trash fills up during a longer stay
- Guests can also take trash to the local drop-off point themselves at no charge
- **How to respond:** "We can arrange an extra trash-out for $50, or you're welcome to take it to the local drop-off point yourself."

---

## Mid-Stay Clean
- **Fee:** Standard cleaning fee for that property
- Available on request for longer stays
- **How to respond:** "We can arrange a mid-stay clean for you at our standard cleaning fee. Just let us know what day works best."

---
