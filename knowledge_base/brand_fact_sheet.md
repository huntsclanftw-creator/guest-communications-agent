## Universal House Rules

These apply to every property:

- Booker must be **25 years or older** and submit to third-party ID verification
- No parties or events — immediate eviction without refund
- **How to respond:** "You are welcome to have a few guests over as long as you do not exceed the maximum capacity of the property or stay overnight. Please keep noise to a minimum, and be aware that if it turns into a party, we may have to cancel your reservation."
- No indoor smoking (includes e-cigarettes, vapes, marijuana) — **$500 fine** *(exception: Clover House TX, where smoking is permitted)*
- **How to respond:** "Smoking inside of the property is strictly prohibited and could result in additional fees and fines. Please keep all activities outdoors and clean up after yourself."
- No unregistered guests — immediate eviction without refund
- **How to respond:** "All overnight guests must be included on the reservation and pay any extra guest fees."
- No illegal substances on premises
- No firearms on premises (any found remitted to local police)
- AC: set to 74°F in summer and 68°F in winter when leaving the property
- Security cameras present on all properties — do not tamper
- Dishes in dishwasher with provided soap pod before checkout
- Return moved furniture to original position; TV remotes to correct rooms
- Remove and properly dispose of all trash

## Pets

- Most properties are dog-friendly (generally max 2 pets)
- Pet fees vary by property — see individual property fact sheets
- No extra fee for damage unless there actually is damage (Serenity Cabin)
- ESAs are treated the same as pets — allowed, no extra fee unless damage
- Properties are **not set up for infants** (no baby gates, no outlet covers) — James proactively discloses this and lets the guest decide. Exception: Clover House TX has a crib.
- **How to respond:** "Please keep in mind that our properties are not baby- or toddler-proof, so you will need to keep an eye on them to avoid any outlet shocks or sharp table edges."

**How to respond:**
- Pet-friendly property: "Yes, we love our dogs! There is a pet fee that helps with the additional hair cleanup." (confirm fee for that property)
- Non-pet-friendly property: "I am sorry, but the owners of the home have decided to keep it closed to pets."

- **How to respond:** "Thank you for being great guests and reporting this to us. Please provide us with some pictures of how the damage occurred. We will do our best to fix it as cheaply as we can and then let you know how much it will cost."

## Wildlife — Virginia Properties

Black bears are present near several properties. Standard advisory: secure all food and trash outdoors. Bears in this area are generally not aggressive toward humans.

- Serenity Cabin: black bears frequent the area — trash must go in the shed behind the cabin
- Woodward Cottage: bears in area — trash bin clips must be fully secured
- The Overlook: "Bertram the Bear" visits periodically from Shenandoah National Park — dogs must be accompanied outside at all times

**How to respond if a guest reports a bear:** "I am sorry if that scared you. We are in black bear territory. They are generally timid unless threatened. Please do not approach. Secure all trash and food, and wait for him to pass."
